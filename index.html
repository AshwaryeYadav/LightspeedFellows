Program Overview

Join a community of exceptional students building the future of technology

The Lightspeed Fellows Program is designed to provide exceptional students with hands-on experience in venture capital, direct mentorship from our partners, and access to our extensive network of founders and industry leaders.

Direct Mentorship

Work directly with Lightspeed partners who will provide hands-on guidance throughout your fellowship experience.

Network Access

Connect with fellow students, alumni, and professionals across the technology ecosystem.

Career Opportunities

Exclusive access to internships, full-time roles, and startup opportunities within our network.

Learning Resources

Workshops, events, and educational content designed to accelerate your professional development.

Hands-On Partner Involvement

Our partners will be actively involved with the cohort, providing direct mentorship and guidance throughout the program.

Who Should Apply

We're looking for exceptional students passionate about technology and innovation

Eligibility Criteria

  • Currently enrolled undergraduate or graduate student
  • Pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field
  • Strong academic performance and demonstrated technical skills
  • Passion for technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship
  • Leadership experience or potential for leadership
  • Available to participate in program activities and events

What We Look For

While we welcome applications from all grade levels, we typically select upperclassmen and graduate students who have demonstrated strong technical foundations and leadership potential. We value diversity of thought, background, and experience.
